Marbella-Spain

Playa de la Fontanilla beach

Playa de la Fontanilla in Marbella is a stunning coastal heaven where golden sands meet the sparkling Mediterranean waters. This pristine beach near the town center offers a serene escape, perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and seaside strolls. With beachfront amenities and vibrant dining options, it's an idyllic destination to unwind and soak in the beauty of the Spanish coast.

Casanis Bistrot Marbella

For a delightful fusion of French cuisine and Mediterranean flavors in Marbella's Old Town, head to Casanis Bistrot. With its inviting ambiance and diverse menu featuring classics like steak frites and fresh seafood, it's the perfect spot for a memorable dining experience. Don't miss their carefully curated wine list and signature cocktails. Casanis Bistrot promises warm hospitality and exceptional food, making it a must-visit for food lovers in Marbella.

Yacht Club

In Marbella, indulging in the yacht club scene is not just a popular pastime but also one of the finest experiences the area has to offer. With its glamorous atmosphere and stunning waterfront views, Marbella's yacht clubs attract both locals and visitors seeking luxury and leisure. Whether you're lounging on a yacht, savoring gourmet cuisine at waterfront restaurants, or enjoying exclusive events and parties, the yacht clubs of Marbella offer a taste of the high life on the Mediterranean coast. It's undoubtedly one of the best ways to immerse yourself in the opulence and allure of Marbella's coastal lifestyle.
