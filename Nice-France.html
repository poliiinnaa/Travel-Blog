Nice-France

Cours Saleya Market

In Nice, the markets offer a vibrant blend of culture and fresh produce. At the Cours Saleya Market, you can purchase fresh seafood and enjoy the lively atmosphere, filled with local vendors and traditional Provençal products. This market provides a unique cultural experience with its colorful stalls and diverse offerings, making it a must-visit for both locals and tourists.

Plage Publique de Castel

The most popular beach in Nice is Castel Plage. Located at the eastern end of the Promenade des Anglais, near the base of Castle Hill (Colline du Château), Castel Plage is well-known for its scenic views and more exclusive atmosphere. It offers a mix of pebbly and sandy areas, clear waters, and excellent facilities, including a restaurant and lounge area. This beach is favored by those looking for a slightly more upscale and tranquil beach experience compared to the bustling Promenade des Anglais.

Vieille ville-Old town

The Vieille Ville, or Old Town, in Nice is a charming historic district known for its narrow cobblestone streets, colorful buildings, and lively atmosphere. This vibrant area is home to bustling markets, quaint cafes, and boutique shops, offering visitors a taste of authentic Niçois culture. With its picturesque squares and hidden gems around every corner, the Vieille Ville is a delightful destination for exploring centuries-old architecture and soaking up the vibrant local vibe.
